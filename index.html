About the Program

Unpack Philly is a guided tour of Philadelphia's civic communities, designed to help new residents learn about and feel at home in Philly. For two months, each week you'll receive an email from someone who loves this city, is involved in an active community that they would like to share, and an invitation to a related event that's happening that week. It could be anything from a hobby club, to a professional meetup, or a volunteer group. You'll get a personal introduction and, if you're interested, a group of other new Philadelphians to attend the event with.
